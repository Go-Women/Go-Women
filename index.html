Project Synopsis

The Inductee Selection application is a one-stop shop portal for The National Women's Hall of Fame. The system aims to provide a more well-documented, streamlined process for the selection of nominees and reduce the manual labor of all stakeholders. The portal will be used by The National Women's Hall of Fame staff, judges, and the average user who wants to make a nomination. All tasks and forms would be collected in one area with user-friendly navigation.

Project Goals

The goal of this project is to reduce the process time frame caused by the manual tasks and create an application that can automate parts of the Inductee Selection Process. Another goal is to reduce as much bias as possible when selecting inductees.

Project Scope

The project will mainly focus on the matching of judges to nominees. We will not be focusing on the gathering of nominations or the processing of nominations by judges.
